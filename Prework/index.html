Welcome to My Site

I was born and raised in Baltimore, MD , one of the largest cities in Maryland.
While there I witnessed many facets of urban life that molded me into who I am today. As a child I showed interest in being an artist from a young age.

Things I love about my hometown

  • Crabcakes!
  • Ravens Football!
  • Chicken Boxes! (A great delicacy!)
  • Summertime at the Inner Harbor

Education

I graduated from the illustrious Morgan State University with a Bachelor's Degree in Civil Engineering. Fun Fact, Morgan State is actually marked as a National Treasure by the United States Government. Current President Joe Biden once was a commencement speaker for graduation.

Hobbies

I work as a freelancer doing graphic and web design. In addition to that, I produce, record, and engineer my own music. I have an album released on March 1st called Do More Math. My influences include Wu-Tang Clan, Tribe Called Quest, and Nas.
